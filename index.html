Distorted Decade are an energetic function band who can cater for any event requiring quality entertainment and totally live music.

No event is too big or too small. Recently the band has performed at a generous selection of weddings, a Waitrose awards ceremony, Ministry of Defence balls, various private parties and some great local music venues.

Music covered is from the 50's right through to the music of the present day, and is all delivered in true Distorted Decade style with plenty of groove to ensure your guests can't resist getting up and having a boogie.
